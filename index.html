Heyo, my name is Cole! Thank you for stopping by! I am an enthusiastic marketer with a passion for all things digital. I am currently based out of Buffalo, NY working as a Digital Marketer for mid-sized, but mighty, agency called Cypress North. I have a B.B.A. with a concentration in Marketing from Loyola University Maryland.

As a Digital Marketer at Cypress North, I do it all: account management, project management, client communication, strategic planning, campaign execution, reporting; you name it, I'll figure it out. While my task list may vary widely from day to day, my primary responsibility is my clients' satisfaction. I am well versed in Search Engine Marketing (SEM), Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Paid Social, and advanced tracking setups. I am a stickler for tracking results and proving the benefit of digital efforts over traditional media. I spend my days managing multiple client relationships and the unique digital initiatives each one brings to the table. Although my clients share ad platforms, no two clients are the same. What may work for one, may not work for another; that is the beauty (and challenge!) of digital marketing.

Speaking of said clients, I've been afforded the opportunity to work with many genuinely awesome folks across several industries, both B2C and B2B, local and international.
