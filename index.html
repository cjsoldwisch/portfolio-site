Heyo, my name is Cole - thank you for stopping by! I am an enthusiastic marketer with a passion for all things digital. I am currently based out of Buffalo, NY working as a Digital Marketing Manager for mid-sized, but mighty, agency called Cypress North. I specialize in the B2B space driving actionable leads for my clients that impact their bottom line - whether it be form fills, e-commerce sales, or offline phone calls. My primary responsibility is my clients' satisfaction.

In my current role at Cypress North, I have transitioned away from task-based execution into a position in which I act as the overall results manager for a selection of B2B clients. I manage client relationships and the unique digital initiatives each one brings to the table. I develop and oversee strategies, and closely track results in constant search for optimizations or process improvements. Digital marketing changes fast (and often) - it is my job to ensure my clients are prepped for, and adhere to, those changes. Although my clients share ad platforms, no two clients are the same. What may work for one, may not work for another; that is the beauty (and challenge!) of digital marketing.

I am well versed in Search Engine Marketing (SEM - Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising), Lead Generation & E-Commerce Sales, Paid Social (specifically LinkedIn), and reporting (analytics and advanced tracking). I am a stickler for tracking results and proving the benefit of digital efforts over traditional media.

My work would not be possible without my wonderful clients. Although I currently specialize in B2B lead generation, I've been afforded the opportunity to work with many genuinely awesome folks across several industries, both B2C and B2B, local and international.
